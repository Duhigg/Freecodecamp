Utrecht is a beautiful City full of canals

    Here are some fun facts about Utrecht!
  • It’s the 4th largest city in the Netherlands
  • It has the largest student population in the country (70,000). This is also one of the many reasons this city is so fun to visit!
  • Dick Bruna, the creator of the famous cartoon character ‘Miffy’ is from Utrecht. You’ll find Miffy throughout the city.
  • It’s central location makes it easy to access from cities across the country. Perfect for day or weekend trips!
  • Utrecht has been boasted as ‘One of the World’s 10 Unsung Places’ by Lonely Planet, declared the ‘New Amsterdam’ by CNN, and BBC Travel titled it one of the ‘Five Happiest Places to Live’!
